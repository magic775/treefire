Buffy & Fluffy

A café and restaurant offering breakfast, brunch, and premium drinks. It serves a distinctive selection of breakfast items such as toast, Turkish falafel, cheese omelette, and the special tuna sandwich with mayo from Buffy & Fluffy, along with other light meals. The café also offers a variety of desserts, including cheesecake, cookies, and Oreo desserts. For drinks, it serves premium beverages such as Kinder Frappé, iced lattes, and espresso freddo, as well as other modern and traditional drinks. Don't miss the opportunity to enjoy your breakfast and favorite drink at Buffy & Fluffy to start your day fresh.

Satalana

A café and restaurant offering a variety of quick sandwiches. It has a special selection of drinks, including smoothies, iced coffee, lattes, and premium coffee, as well as natural fruit juices. It also serves sandwiches made from sausage, kofta, liver, and hawawshi. It's an ideal place to enjoy a quiet atmosphere and finish your meals in peace and comfort.

Acadia

A café and mall offering a unique selection of drinks, fast food, and special desserts. It serves a variety of hot drinks, such as Turkish and French coffee, hot chocolate, lattes, espresso, Café Mocha, and Nescafé Galaxy, as well as tea, sahlab, herbs, and others. Desserts include Oreo tagine with ice cream, waffles, and cheesecake. Acadia also offers a variety of fast food, such as chicken dishes like Chicken Afernes, Chicken Alking, schnitzel, and fajita, along with diet meals like steak and boiled meat with vegetables. It also offers special soups and salads. It’s an ideal place to spend time with your family and children, enjoying the kids' area and spending your holiday.

Lamira

A café and restaurant where you can enjoy upscale drinks and meals on the Nile Corniche. It offers a variety of drinks such as soda smoothies like Red Berry Smoothie and Energy Smoothie, as well as fresh juices like Banana Brisky and Mango Berry. For hot drinks, it serves hot chocolate, Nutella hot drinks, sahlab with nuts, and herbal drinks, in addition to various types of coffee such as espresso, Turkish coffee, French coffee, iced coffee, and caramel macchiato. As for meals, it serves a variety of dishes to suit different tastes, such as burgers, steaks, kofta, grilled fillet, Mexican spiced chicken, and Sweet Red Curry chicken. Lamira also offers appetizers, soups, and stews such as meat with onions, kebab, and other varieties. The desserts include Molten Cake, Gedo’s Tagine, and Om Ali with cream, among others.

A_class Restaurant and Café

A café and restaurant offering the best drinks, desserts, and meals with excellent service.
